## Long-Form Campaign Copy

You meant to take a five-minute break.

Then one tab became five, one video became three, and "I'll use Codex later" quietly turned into another day with the work untouched and the quota you meant to use going nowhere.

Token Harbor was made for that kind of Codex procrastination.

When you finally open Codex and start solving, writing, building, or organizing, every token you actually use becomes Sail Power: fuel for a fleet waiting to leave the harbor. Finish a task and your work moves forward. Build a little momentum and your harbor grows with you.

Build ports, send vessels to sea, catch fish, upgrade your fleet, and prepare for raiders and rough weather. Token usage that once felt abstract becomes a living maritime world you can watch develop between tasks.

Token Harbor does not do the work for you, and it does not ask you to burn tokens for no reason. It simply gives the work you already need to do a visible reward and one more reason to begin.

Stop leaving Codex for later. Start the work. Send the first boat out.
